This Private Oceanfront Home Is Perfect For A Couple Or Small Family!

This lovely home in Timber Cove, just north of Jenner, is located on a secluded, private bluff and nestled on a 2-acre meadow facing its own private cove with breathtaking whitewater views. Experience the solitude and tranquility of this unique oceanfront home, or take advantage of the abundant activities offered by the region: winetasting, sea kayaking, fishing, boating, hiking, dining, and exploring secluded coves and tidepools. At night, settle down in the living room before a toasty fire, or enjoy Ocean Hideaway's hot tub while marveling at the star-studded sky and listening to the crashing surf.
