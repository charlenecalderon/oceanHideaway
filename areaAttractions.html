Fishing

When in season, visitors with valid fishing licenses can fish for rock fish, salmon, perch, steelhead and smelt.

Abalone Hunting

The Timber Cove area is world-renowned for Red Abalone. Abalone hunting is seasonal and highly regulated by the California Department of Fish and Wildlife. It requires a license and other rigid requirements.
